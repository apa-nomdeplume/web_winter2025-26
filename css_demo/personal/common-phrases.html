Common phrases:

Greetings, friend: Kvetha fricai
Manin! Wyrda! Hugin!: Memory! Fate! Thought!
May good fortune rule over you: Atra esterní ono thelduin
May peace live in your heart: Atra mor'ranr lífa unin hjarta onr
May luck and happiness follow you: Atra guliä un ilian tauthr ono
May the stars watch over you: Atra evarínya ono varda
May you be happy: Sé ono waise ilia
May you find peace: Sé mor'ranr ono finna
May your swords stay sharp!: Sé onr sverdar sitja hvass!
