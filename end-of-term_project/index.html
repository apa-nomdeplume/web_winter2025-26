Welcome!


Welcome to a tour of Alagaësia. This website tells about the ancient language, the language of the land, about the characters in Alagaësia, the humans as well as the many other races. There is a photo gallery, with images of dragons, a rune from the elves' script, the Liduen Kvaedhí, and a map of the land of Alagaësia.

The Inheritance Cycle is a series written by Christopher Paolini. The first book, Eragon, was published in 2002. The second book, Eldest, was published in 2005, the third, Brisingr, in 2008, and the final, Inheritance, in 2011. The main character, Eragon, is a farm boy who finds a mysterious blue stone in the forest while he's hunting. This stone ends up hatching into a dragon, which begins the whirl of events that launch Eragon into the role of the only of the legendary Dragon Riders that is not under the evil king, Galbatorix's watch. It is up to Eragon to release the Empire and the whole of Alagaësia from Galbatorix's wrath.

Warning: This site contains spoilers for each of the four books.
