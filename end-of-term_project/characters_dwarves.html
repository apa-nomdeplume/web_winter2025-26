Characters: A Guide



Dwarves:

Dwarves (Knurlan in Dwarvish, singular knurla and Dvergar in the Ancient Language once known to all creatures) are one of the only races native to Alagaësia. Known as lovers of rock and stone, dwarves have a long lifespan and often live in large, underground cities in the Beor Mountains. One of the most famous dwarf cities is located in Farthen Dûr ("Our Father" in Dwarvish). The city itself, Tronjheim, was the secret hiding place of the Varden up until the Battle under Farthen Dûr. The dwarves have many clans such as the Dûrgrimst Az Sweldn rak Anhûin and the Dûrgrimst Ingeitum. With the fall of the Broddring Empire, the Dwarves were accepted into the Dragon Riders along with the Urgals.
