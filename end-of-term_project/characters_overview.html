Characters: A Guide



Overview:


There are 5 races of sentient beings in Alagaësia. The dwarves and the dragons, which are native to Alagaësia, and the elves, humans, and the Urgals, which came from a different land. They each have their own unique traits and customs, which will be mentioned in the following pages. Humans (I'm assuming) are already known about, so there won't be a page on them.

Enjoy!
